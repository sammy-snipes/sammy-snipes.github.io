## Misc. Projects
- **[Autograd Engine](https://github.com/sammy-snipes/slowgrad)**: Einsum is really all you need. I thought it would be funny to build an autograd engine around that idea. Still a work in progress. The actual engine is pretty small, but theres I wrote a bunch of unecessarry goofy code just to make it feel like pytorch.  
- **Novel Generator**: When the GPT hullabaloo was afoot I made a GPT-2 style transformer that made novels written in japanese. I wanted to get more familiar with the architecture.I couldnt really read JP very well when I made it so I couldnt asess the model that well. The base model was trained on  [ner-wikipedia-dataset](https://github.com/stockmarkteam/ner-wikipedia-dataset) and [TwitterCorpus](https://github.com/tmu-nlp/TwitterCorpus) and then I fine tuned it on 60,000 novels I scraped. 
- **Video Game Aimbot**: I made a CNN to cheat in Overwatch when I was in grad school. It *worked*, kinda. It could aim better than me on Soldier 76 and Mc'Cree, but making it switch targets was hard so it didnt work that well. I was generating/labeling training data myself which was a pain in the ass so I abandonded the bot in her half finished state. Call it a *proof of concept* and move on....
